Jimbando The Drunken Clown

A Chaotic Adventure in Maintaining the Buzz!

How to Play

  • Use arrow keys to navigate Jimbando through the maze
  • Collect beers (5% buzz) and whiskey bottles (10% buzz)
  • Keep your buzz level above 0% or it's game over!
  • Watch out for walls - even drunk clowns can't walk through them
